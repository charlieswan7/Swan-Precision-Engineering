About Us

Local fabrication and welding with a focus on dependable results.

Who We Are

Swan Precision Engineering is based in Skryne, County Meath, providing steel fabrication, welding, and repair services for a wide range of practical projects.

We work with customers who need strong, well-finished metalwork and a service that is clear, reliable, and straightforward from the first conversation.

What Matters to Us

  • Precision in the detail
  • Durability in the finished work
  • Practical solutions for real use
  • Honest communication throughout the job

Our Approach

Good metalwork starts with understanding the job properly.

Whether the work is a new fabrication, a repair, or a bespoke piece, the goal is the same: build something that performs well, lasts, and suits the space or application it is made for.
